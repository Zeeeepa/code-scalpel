Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

3D Tech Solutions LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates Code Scalpel, an MCP server toolkit for AI-driven code analysis. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software and services.

Key Point: Code Scalpel runs locally on your machine. We do not have access to, process, or store the code you analyze — your code never leaves your environment.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Purchase a Pro or Enterprise license
  • Create an account on our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us for support
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This may include your name, email address, company name, billing information, and any other information you choose to provide.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information including:

  • IP address and general location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device information

What We Do NOT Collect

Code Scalpel is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do NOT collect, access, or process your source code
  • We do NOT have visibility into what files you analyze
  • We do NOT track which MCP tools you use
  • We do NOT collect analysis results or security findings

All code analysis happens locally on your machine. The MCP server runs entirely in your environment.

Optional Telemetry (Opt-In Only)

We may offer optional, opt-in telemetry in future versions to help improve Code Scalpel. If implemented:

  • Telemetry will be disabled by default
  • You must explicitly enable it
  • Only aggregated, anonymized usage statistics would be collected
  • No source code or analysis results would ever be transmitted

We will update this policy before enabling any telemetry features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process license purchases and provide customer support
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, license keys, updates)
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe), email services, and hosting providers who help us operate our business
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to our website.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Port your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@codescalpel.dev.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Essential cookies are required for the website to function; analytics cookies help us understand how you use our site.

Children's Privacy

Code Scalpel is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
