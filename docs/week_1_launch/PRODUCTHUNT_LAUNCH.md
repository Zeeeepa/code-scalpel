## Contingency Plans

### If ranking is too low (outside Top 50)
1. **Day 1 evening:** Send email to all beta testers asking for upvotes
2. **Day 2 morning:** Share more on social media
3. **Day 2 afternoon:** Consider whether it's worth continuing or pivoting focus

### If you get critical feedback
1. **Respond respectfully** (thank them for the feedback)
2. **Acknowledge valid points** (don't get defensive)
3. **Offer solution** (fix it, create roadmap item, explain limitation)
4. **Follow up** (let them know when fix is live)

### If there's a technical issue
1. **Acknowledge immediately** ("We're aware and investigating")
2. **Provide timeline** ("Fix coming in 2 hours")
3. **Over-communicate** (update every 30 min)
4. **Thank people for patience** (they'll stick with you)
