1. **Subject line matters**
   - ‚ùå "New tool to check out"

   - ‚ùå "Dear Developer"

3. **Keep it short**
   - ‚ùå 500+ words
   - ‚úÖ 200-300 words
   
4. **Clear value proposition**
   - ‚ùå "Join our beta program"
   - ‚úÖ "Try free, get early access, influence the roadmap"

5. **Easy CTA**
   - ‚ùå "Schedule a call to discuss"
   - ‚úÖ "Click here to sign up"

6. **Time it right**
