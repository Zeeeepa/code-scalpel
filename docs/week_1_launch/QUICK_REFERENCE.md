## ‚ö° QUICK TIPS

**Blog Posts**
- Don't write from scratch‚Äîuse templates
- Customize examples for your situation
- Publish to Dev.to + Medium (double reach)
- Share on Twitter, Reddit, Slack

**Demo Video**
- Record voiceover separately
- Edit with OBS or Camtasia
- Hire editor if short on time ($100-300)
- 2 minutes is perfect (longer = fewer views)

**Beta Outreach**
- Tier 1 (warm): 20-30 people, ~80% response
- Tier 2 (warm network): 20-30, ~40% response
- Tier 3 (community): 50-100, ~5-10% response
- Follow up after 3-5 days if no response

**ProductHunt**
- Post Friday at 7 AM PT (peak time)
- Be responsive (reply within 30 min)
- Engage with other products too
- Don't oversell, be authentic

**Metrics**
- Update daily at same time (e.g., 6 PM)
- Compare to target (not perfection)
- Celebrate wins publicly
- Document what drove each spike
